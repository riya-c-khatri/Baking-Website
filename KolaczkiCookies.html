Kolaczki Cookies

Ingredients (For 2-3 Dozen Kolaczki Cookies):

⅔ cup sugar
1 cup butter
8 ounces cream cheese
A pinch of salt
1 egg
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed
⅓ cup jam of your choice (apricot and strawberry recommended)
Powdered sugar, for decorating

Instructions:

Note: The mixing in this recipe can be done by hand, using a hand mixer, or in a stand mixer (recommended).

  • Cream the sugar with the butter and cream cheese until combined and smooth.
  • Mix in the salt, egg, and vanilla extract until combined.
  • Add the flour and mix until combined. Note: The dough will be slightly sticky to the touch. If it sticks excessively to your hands, sprinkle some more flour.
  • Refrigerate the dough for 1 hour.
  • Preheat the oven to 325°F and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  • On a floured surface, roll the dough out to ⅛-inch thickness and cut into 2 ½-inch squares. If the dough sticks, sprinkle some more flour.
  • Spoon a small dollop of jam onto the center of each square. Fold two opposite corners together and gently press to seal.
  • Bake for 22 minutes. Note: Cook time may vary depending on the oven.
  • Optional: Dust the kolaczki cookies with powdered sugar.
  • Allow the cookies to cool slightly before serving, as the jam will be very hot.
  • Enjoy!
