Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients (For 12 Cinnamon Rolls):

For the Cinnamon Roll Dough:

⅓ cup warm milk, more as necessary
1 tsp yeast
2 cups King Arthur All Purpose Flour, more as necessary
¼ cup sugar
A pinch of salt
3 tbsp butter
1 egg

For the Cinnamon Roll Filling:

4 tbsp butter
⅝ cup brown sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon

For the Cream Cheese Icing:

4 tbsp butter
1.3 oz cream cheese
¾ cup powdered sugar
A pinch of salt
¼ tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp milk (if needed for desired consistency)

Instructions:

  • Warm the milk. Mix in the yeast and wait 5-10 minutes until slightly frothy.
  • Add the flour, sugar, salt, butter, and egg to the bowl of a stand mixer and mix for 2 minutes until incorporated. Then add the milk-yeast mixture little by little.
  • Note: Add a few drops of milk if the dough is tough and not binding. If the dough is too sticky, sprinkle some flour.
  • Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes in the stand mixer.
  • Oil a large bowl to place the dough in. Cover and place the dough in a warm place for 1-2 hours so it can rise.
  • Roll the dough out into a 12-inch x 10-inch rectangle with an approximate thickness of ¼ inch. The width and height of the rectangle can vary at this stage.
  • Melt the butter and spread over the dough. Combine the brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over the dough.
  • From the long end of the dough, roll it up. Then cut 12 cinnamon rolls out.
  • Place the cinnamon rolls in a buttered pan. Cover and let them rise for another 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  • Preheat the oven to 350°F and bake the cinnamon rolls for 15-20 minutes. When they are golden brown, they are ready.
  • Mix all the icing ingredients together with a hand mixer. Even if it seems like the ingredients are not binding, keep mixing for about 5 minutes.
  • Note: If the icing looks too thick to spread, add a few drops of milk.
  • As soon as the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, spread them with icing.
  • Enjoy!
