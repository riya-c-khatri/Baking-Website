Mini Blueberry Cheesecakes

Ingredients (For 12 Mini Cheesecakes):

For the Graham Cracker Base:

5 graham crackers
2 tsp sugar
2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

For the Cheesecake:

8 oz cream cheese
3 tsp cornstarch
3 tsp sour cream
¼ cup sugar
3 tsp heavy cream
1 tsp vanilla bean paste
1 egg

For Decorating:

Blueberries

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 300°F. Spray a mini cupcake tin with non-stick baking spray.
  • Using a food processor, pulse the graham crackers until no large crumbs are left.
  • Transfer the crumbs to a small mixing bowl. Add & mix in the sugar, followed by the butter.
  • Using measuring spoons, scoop 3 ½ tsp of the base mixture into each mold. Use the back of a spoon to press down on the mixture to ensure it is firmly compact.
  • Bake the crust for 7 minutes.
  • As the crust bakes, make the filling:
  • Using a hand mixer/large bowl or a stand mixer, whisk the cream cheese, cornstarch, and sour cream until creamy.
  • Add the sugar & mix until combined. Then, add the heavy cream & mix until combined.
  • Add the vanilla bean paste. For a stronger taste of vanilla, add an extra ½ tsp of the paste.
  • Add the egg & mix well until combined.
  • Using measuring spoons, scoop 5 tsp of the filling on top of each crust.
  • Bake for 20 minutes until the cheesecakes are set. (Note: baking time will vary depending on your oven).
  • Let the cheesecakes cool for 45 minutes at room temperature. Then cover them & refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  • When you are ready to serve, remove them from the pan and decorate with blueberries or other toppings of your choice.
  • Enjoy!
